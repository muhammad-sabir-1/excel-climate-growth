4 Testing & reporting — the 80/20 framework

We run the account on one disciplined framework:

  • 80% of spend stays on proven winners; 20% runs a rotating bench of new tests — angles, offers, and audiences.
  • We kill weak creatives early on leading signals, and let promising ones run to confirm on qualified-lead cost. Every week we scale winners, cut the rest, and refill from the backlog.
  • Weekly reporting focused on lead quality: spend, leads, cost per lead, qualified leads and booked appointments where available, best/worst campaigns, and next-week changes.

Month 1 — foundation
